AI - Made by Ethan Griffin

Controls

mouse - used to control player boid in pursuit and evade
mouse left click - spawn boids
mouse right click - delete boids

Q - delete all boids
C - Toggle containment/wrapping

0 - Seek
1 - Flee
2 - Pursuit
3 - Evade
4 - Arrive
5 - Wander
6 - Path following/group path following

F - Flocking
L - Leader following
